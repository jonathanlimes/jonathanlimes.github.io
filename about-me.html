ABOUT
I am currently working as a law enforcement officer in Singapore. In the past three years, I did criminal investigation and solved a range of violent hurt and financial crimes. I also led a team of first response officers and learnt quite a lot handling time-critical emergencies.

On the side, I do full stack web development. I picked up programming and started to build web apps because I wanted to give my ideas real form. I didn't want them to remain in a notebook, delivering no value to the world. In a span of three months, I built a machine learning productivity app, a sandbox incident management app, a crowdsourcing app for line-sitters, and a real-time two-player JavaScript game. I am happy to help people build things for free, just so that I can learn and improve.

I often think about how technology can free people to focus on higher-order work, and I am learning how to make that happen. When I'm free I like to read about productivity, cook, biohack, and do olympic weightlifting. Also a closet philosopher.
