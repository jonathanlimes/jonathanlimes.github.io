StandIn
Crowdsourcing app for line-sitters
StandIn
StandIn is a web app that connects the time-strapped with the cash-strapped. It lets users post requests for other users to stand in line for them, for a fee. Once completed, payments can be made seamlessly on the site.

Built with Ruby on Rails and Braintree SDK. I was part of a team of three who worked on this project. We finished it in four days, just a week after learning Ruby on Rails and PostgreSQL. The development process also included a User Experience Research component where we conducted user interviews and developed personas and user stories.
