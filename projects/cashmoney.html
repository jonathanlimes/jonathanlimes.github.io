Cash Money
Cash Money is a real-time two-player game. Two players compete to earn the largest amount of money in thirty seconds by collecting red packets sprawled across the game grid. Unfortunately, each red packet slows them down. The players can also collect non-valuable oranges that speed them up, and also risk bumping into firecrackers which will make them lose money and have them return to their original starting position.

Built with JavaScript, HTML5 Canvas, and jQuery. I built this game to consolidate my understanding of JavaScript. It took about four days. I found most of the graphics and audio files on open source libraries, but designed the game map myself.
