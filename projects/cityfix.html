CityFix
CityFix is a incident management app to help city residents improve their neighbourhood. If residents witness something that needs fixing, such as a cracked pavement or a stained wall, they can post an issue request on CityFix and view their request updates on the app. Public Works Officers will receive an overview of all public works requests from all resident users on a map, and can plan their work more effectively.

Built with Node.js, Express.js, MongoDB, and Google Maps JavaScript API. Coming from an operations background, I wanted test my learning of full stack web development by redesigning the way first-response and public works departments manage their incidents. CityFix was the final product after coding for a week.
