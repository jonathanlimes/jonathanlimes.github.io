Telder
Telder is a productivity note-taking app. Take your notes, and have them all summarized and categorized instantly. It also sports a real-time collaborative editor for you to work on notes simultaneously with your team members.

Built with Ruby on Rails and Natural Language Processing APIs.I wanted to work on a product that would combine my interests in Productivity and Machine Learning. I took myself through a full development process which included user experience research, wireframing, database architecture, and front-end design.
